Impact Report

Our planet is suffering from the impacts of climate change and pollution. Here are some statistics:

  • Carbon dioxide levels in the atmosphere have risen by 40% since pre-industrial times
  • Over 1 Million species are at risk of extinction due to human activities
  • Plastic waste in the ocean is expected to outweigh fish by 2050

To learn more about the impact of climate change and pollution, visit https://moef.gov.in/en/rules-and-regulations/environment-protection/
